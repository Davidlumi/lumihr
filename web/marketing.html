Built on trust

A benchmark you can put your name to

lumi is deliberately conservative with your data and other members’ — so every number is one you can defend.

Anonymity comes first

If fewer than 5 organisations sit behind a figure, lumi hides it — no individual organisation’s data can be worked out.

Medians, not averages

Every comparison is a median or percentile across valid peer answers, so one unusual organisation can’t skew your read.

Every figure is cited

Percentile, peer group and sample size travel with every number — on screen, in exports, and in the board pack.

We flag, you decide

lumi surfaces what’s worth a look and explains it in plain English — it never tells you what your reward strategy should be.
