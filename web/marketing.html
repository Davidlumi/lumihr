Data & trust

Deliberately conservative with every number

Reward data is sensitive. lumi is built so that every figure is one you — and we — can defend.

Anonymity comes first

If fewer than 5 organisations sit behind a figure, it is never shown — on screen, in exports, or in print. No individual organisation's data can be worked out.

Medians, not averages

Every comparison is a median or percentile across valid peer answers, so one unusual organisation can't skew your read.

Every figure is cited

Percentile, peer group and sample size travel with every number — there is no such thing as an unattributed figure in lumi.

Your data stays yours

Member data is used for one thing: the benchmark you all share. It is never sold, never passed to third parties, and handled under UK GDPR.

A UK pool, for UK decisions

Every organisation in the benchmark is a UK employer. No blending with overseas markets that pay differently.

No agenda at review time

lumi has nothing to sell you when the decision lands — it surfaces the gap, cites the evidence, and stops there. The strategy stays yours.

FAQ

The questions every HR team asks

Where does the data come from?+
From members. lumi is a benchmarking co-operative: every organisation in the pool is a real UK employer that submits its own reward data. Nothing is scraped, purchased or modelled — which is why every figure can cite the number of organisations behind it.
Is our data safe?+
Yes — and provably so. Any figure resting on fewer than 5 organisations is suppressed everywhere: on screen, in exports, in board packs. Comparisons are medians and percentiles, so no single organisation's numbers can be reverse-engineered. Your data is used only for the shared benchmark, never sold, and handled under UK GDPR.
Do you benchmark salaries?+
No — and that's deliberate. lumi benchmarks the rest of reward: benefits, time off, pension, wellbeing, recognition, and the policies and practices around pay — the things a salary survey never covers. It's how you tell the board whether your whole package is competitive, not just base pay.
How is this different from a reward survey?+
A survey is a snapshot in one fixed peer cut, delivered months later as a PDF. lumi is a living benchmark: your position updates as members contribute, signals surface what's worth a look each time you open it, you can re-cut the peer group yourself in seconds, and every figure is cited — so it survives boardroom scrutiny without a consultant on standby.
Do we need to integrate anything?+
No. lumi is a guided questionnaire — roughly an hour of answers, nothing to connect. Because we benchmark reward practices and policies (not individual employee records), there's no HRIS integration, no data pulled from your systems, and far less for IT and InfoSec to review.
Do we have to contribute our own data?+
Yes — that's the co-operative deal, and it's what keeps the benchmark real. You only see comparisons for questions you've answered yourself, and "not applicable" is a valid answer. Most teams find the first meaningful comparison appears within minutes of starting.
How long does it take to get value?+
Your first answer produces a live comparison immediately. A focused hour of data entry typically unlocks a meaningful position across the core domains — and the board pack can be generated the same day.
What does it cost?+
A single annual membership — a fraction of what a traditional reward survey costs to repeat, and far less than a consultant. Because it's a co-operative, everyone shares the cost of the pool. We'll confirm your exact figure on the demo call once we understand your size. No obligation either way.
