# lumi.benchmark — AI Insights Terms

**Version 1.0 · Effective 28 June 2026**

## What "AI Insights" are

AI Insights are short, written interpretations of **your own benchmark figures** —
for example a plain-English read of how a reward domain sits against your peer group,
or commentary on a single metric. They are generated by a large language model and are
**descriptions of your data, not advice**. They never introduce figures that aren't
already on your screen, and every output passes an automated grounding check before you
see it; if it fails, a rule-based summary built only from your numbers is shown instead.
Each insight is shown with a notice that it is AI-generated.

## What data is used

To generate an insight, lumi sends the model **only already-computed, aggregated and
derived figures** for the relevant view — domain-level position counts, named benchmark
metrics with their percentile and sample-size figures, prevalence figures, and the size
of your peer comparison group. **Individual salaries and base-pay levels are not used.**
We never send raw submissions, another organisation's data, or personal data about
individuals beyond the organisation-level figures you have entered.

## The AI provider (sub-processor)

Insights are generated by **Anthropic PBC**, our AI sub-processor, under a data-processing
agreement. Anthropic processes the figures on lumi's instructions to return the generated
text and, under its commercial API terms, **does not retain the data or use it to train its
models**. Anthropic is named in the **Sub-processor List** with the processing purpose and
safeguards.

## Lawful basis and your control

We process these figures to provide AI Insights on the basis of our **legitimate interest**
in offering analytical features within the service (UK GDPR Art. 6(1)(f)); a Legitimate
Interests Assessment is on file. Because the figures are organisational and aggregated, and
the output is descriptive only, this processing is proportionate and low-risk.

- **AI Insights are on by default** as part of your analytical experience.
- **You can turn AI Insights off at any time** in Settings. Doing so stops any further AI
  processing of your data for insights immediately.
- This setting is **per person** — each member of your organisation decides for themselves.
- AI Insights produce descriptive text only and make **no automated decision** with a legal
  or similarly significant effect (UK GDPR Art. 22 does not apply).

## Accuracy

AI-generated summaries are produced automatically and may contain errors. They are a
description of your data, not advice, and must not be relied on as a substitute for
professional reward, legal, financial or HR advice. You remain responsible for all decisions
about your reward arrangements. Where the AI service is unavailable or its output cannot be
verified, lumi shows a plain rule-based summary instead.

## Changes

If we make a material change to how AI Insights work, we may ask you to review the updated
terms. The version in force when your setting was recorded is kept with the date.
