## What "AI Insights" are

AI Insights are short, written interpretations of **your own benchmark figures** —
for example a plain-English read of how a reward domain sits against your peer group,
or commentary on a single metric. They are generated by a large language model and are
**descriptions of your data, not advice**. They never introduce figures that aren't
already on your screen, and every output passes an automated grounding check before you
see it; if it fails, a rule-based summary built only from your numbers is shown instead.

## What data is used

To generate an insight, lumi sends the model **only the already-computed, on-screen
figures** for the relevant view (counts, percentiles, peer-group size, your answers as
displayed) — never raw submissions, never another organisation's data, never personal
data about individuals beyond what you have entered as your own organisation's figures.

## Your choice and your control

- **Your consent is asked for separately** from the general Terms of Use, and is not
  pre-ticked. You can use lumi fully without enabling AI Insights.
- **You can turn AI Insights off at any time** in Settings. Doing so stops any further
  AI processing of your data for insights immediately.
- This consent is **per person** — each member of your organisation decides for
  themselves.
